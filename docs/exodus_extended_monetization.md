## Risk Mitigation

### Regulatory Risks
- **Payment services**: Obtain necessary licenses (money transmitter, etc.)
- **Financial services**: KYC/AML compliance
- **Data protection**: GDPR, CCPA compliance (minimal due to privacy-first design)
- **Gaming**: Age verification, content rating

### Technical Risks
- **P2P network security**: Implement robust encryption and authentication
- **AI agent sandboxing**: Ensure agents cannot escape sandbox
- **Payment security**: Multi-sig wallets, cold storage
- **Game distribution**: DRM and anti-piracy measures

### Market Risks
- **Competition**: Focus on unique value propositions
- **User adoption**: Build strong community and developer ecosystem
- **Regulatory changes**: Stay ahead of regulations
- **Economic downturns**: Diversify revenue streams
